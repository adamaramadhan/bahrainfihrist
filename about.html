About the Project

The Bahrain Fihrist (فهرست البحرين) is a digital bibliography project dedicated to the comprehensive documentation of scholarly resources on Bahraini history. "Fihrist" (فهرست) is the Arabic word for "catalog" or "index," reflecting our mission to create an organized catalog of historical materials related to Bahrain.

This project aims to serve as a central resource for academics, researchers, students, and anyone interested in the rich history of Bahrain. By collecting and categorizing both primary and secondary sources, we hope to facilitate deeper understanding and further research into Bahraini historical studies.

Our bibliography includes a wide range of materials, from ancient manuscripts and archival documents to contemporary academic studies. Each entry is carefully categorized to enable efficient searching and browsing based on time periods, subject matter, and resource type.
