About Bahrain Fihrist

Bahrain Fihrist is a comprehensive bibliographic resource dedicated to works on Bahraini history. Our mission is to compile and organize scholarly resources, primary source materials, and academic studies related to the rich historical legacy of Bahrain.

This bibliography aims to serve as a reference tool for researchers, students, and anyone interested in exploring the history of Bahrain from ancient times to the modern era.

We continuously update this resource to include the latest publications and discoveries in the field.
