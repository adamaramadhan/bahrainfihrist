About Bahrain Fihrist

Bahrain Fihrist serves as a comprehensive digital bibliography focusing on the historical scholarship of Bahrain. This resource aims to collect, organize, and present both primary and secondary sources that illuminate the rich and complex history of this island nation in the Arabian Gulf. From pre-Islamic archaeological findings to contemporary historical analyses, this bibliography seeks to support researchers, students, and anyone interested in understanding Bahrain's historical narrative.

The project recognizes the importance of making historical sources accessible to a global audience while preserving the scholarly rigor necessary for academic research. By organizing materials into clearly defined categories and time periods, users can efficiently navigate through centuries of historical documentation and analysis.

This bibliography is continuously updated as new scholarship emerges and additional historical sources are discovered or digitized. We welcome suggestions for additions and corrections from the scholarly community.
