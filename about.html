About Bahrain Fihrist

Bahrain Fihrist is a specialized online bibliography dedicated to collating and organizing scholarly works and primary sources related to the history of Bahrain. The term "Fihrist" (فهرست) refers to an index or catalogue in Arabic, reflecting our mission to create a comprehensive reference tool for researchers, academics, and anyone interested in Bahraini history.

This project aims to facilitate research by providing a structured, accessible, and continuously updated repository of bibliographic information. We hope to bridge gaps in existing research infrastructure and promote further study of Bahrain's rich historical landscape.

Our bibliography encompasses a wide range of materials, from ancient manuscripts to contemporary academic studies, covering political, social, economic, cultural, and religious aspects of Bahrain's history.
