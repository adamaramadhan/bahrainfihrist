Welcome to Bahrain Fihrist

This website serves as a comprehensive public bibliography on works related to Bahraini history. Our goal is to collect, organize, and make accessible a wide range of sources that document and analyze Bahrain's rich cultural and historical heritage.

The Bahrain Fihrist project aims to be a valuable resource for researchers, students, and anyone interested in the history and culture of Bahrain. By gathering both primary sources and scholarly studies in one place, we hope to facilitate further research and deeper understanding of Bahraini history.

Explore our collections of primary sources and academic studies using the navigation menu above. Each section is organized by category to help you find relevant materials more easily.
