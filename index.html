Welcome to Bahrain Fihrist

This site serves as a comprehensive bibliography of works related to Bahraini history. Our goal is to compile and organize resources that contribute to the understanding of Bahrain's rich historical narrative.

Here you will find carefully categorized collections of both primary and secondary sources that span various periods and themes in Bahraini history. Whether you're a researcher, student, or simply interested in learning about Bahrain's past, our curated lists provide valuable references to aid your exploration.

The bibliography is continuously updated as new works become available or are discovered. We welcome suggestions and contributions from scholars and enthusiasts alike.
